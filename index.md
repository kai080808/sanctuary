# Privacy Policy: Sanctuary

**Last Updated: April 22, 2026**

Sanctuary is built on the principle of **Zero-Knowledge Security**. We believe your data belongs to you, and only you.

### 1. Absolute Privacy by Design
Sanctuary is a local-only application. It does not connect to the internet, and it contains no networking code.
- **No Cloud**: Your passwords never leave your physical device.
- **No Accounts**: We do not collect emails, names, or any personal identifiers.
- **No Tracking**: There are zero analytics, zero cookies, and zero telemetry scripts.

### 2. Hardened Local Encryption
Your data is protected by industry-standard cryptographic protocols:
- **AES-256-GCM**: Military-grade encryption for all stored credentials.
- **Argon2**: Memory-hard key derivation to protect your Master Password against brute-force attacks.
- **Local Vault**: All data is stored in a single, encrypted `sanctuary.vault` file on your machine.

### 3. Zero Data Access
Because we have no servers, it is physically impossible for us to access, view, or share your data. We cannot comply with data requests because we hold no data.

### 4. Your Responsibility
You are the sole guardian of your Master Password and Recovery Key. If these are lost, your data is mathematically unrecoverable. We recommend physical backups for absolute safety.
